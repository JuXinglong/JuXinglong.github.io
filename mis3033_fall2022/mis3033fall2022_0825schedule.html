Expressions

C# Expressions

  • C# contains a number of operators.
  • By combining the operators with variables and literal values, you can create expressions to perform computations.
  • Operators can roughly be classified into three categories:
    • Unary – Act on single operands
    • Binary – Act on two operands
    • Ternary – Act on three operands
  • E.g. +, -, *, /, %
